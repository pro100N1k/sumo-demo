Big data analytics for healthcare industry
SUMO is a gamechanger when it comes to connecting patient care and the unlimited amount of data that hospitals, clinics, and other medical facilities have accumulated over the years.
Now the way that data is accessed and interpreted through SUMO will create a more efficient environment throughout the healthcare system. With the limitations of healthcare staff especially since the pandemic, SUMO can help them handle patient care better and increase the probability of proper diagnoses.

T³ Platform — precise big data analytics
The T³ data science platform creates connections between patients and clinicians in a way that diminishes previous barriers and difficulties in service. With specialisation in musculoskeletal healthcare, SUMO can be a field leader in healthcare data analytics.
Our platform is designed to exceed the expectations of the specific industry and business of its users. Gain back time and eliminate the stress of time-consuming administrative functions. Get precise analytics on patient trends, payment trends, and various datasets. Obtain necessary patient information while remaining within HIPAA guidelines to improve proactive diagnoses.
